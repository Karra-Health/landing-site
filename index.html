Transforming post-surgery care with cutting-edge digital solutions.

At Karra Health, we're on a mission to redefine post-discharge care by developing a state-of-the-art digital care management platform.

Our platform facilitates AI-enhanced remote patient monitoring, patient-provider communications, and care adherence reminders proven to reduce complications and readmission rates.

Backed by a team of medical advisors from world-class healthcare systems, we're dedicated to improving patient recovery and expanding close monitoring to the home.

Key Features
Streamlined for Simplicity: Intuitive features designed with both patient and provider in mind.
Remote Monitoring

Stay connected with patients post-surgery through remote monitoring and real-time patient condition alerts.

EPIC EMR Integrated

Developed with EMR integration in mind, our software appears as a window within EPIC.

Care Adherance Reminders

Automatic reminders sent to patient for medication, vitals taking, and doctors visits.

AI Communication

AI-enhanced patient-clinician communication for reduced clinician workload and early intervention.
